PC Needle Retreat

Come and learn how to get the most out of your Gammill Statler.

Retreat: Star Valley Wyoming - August 16-17, 2017


Register

Schedule

PC Needle Retreats is excited to announce a Creative Studio 7.0 retreat in Star Valley Wyoming taught by Paula Everill and Cindy Gerber-Chavez. 

We will cover all the new and improved features of CS 7.0 including the tool bar customization, pattern management, circular array, text feature, echo, draw features, the new file extension, and much more. 

Bring your laptop for lots of fun in Stand Alone Mode (SAM).

August 16 & 17, 2017 at the Dutch Oven Inn

Price includes two full days of training, accommodations for August 15 & 16, along with Breakfast lunch and dinner on the days of class. 

Space is limited so sign up now for a fun and educational class.

Up to three additional nights of accommodations are available for those participating in the Star Valley Quilt Trail events.

Our Team

Certified Statler Instructors

Cindy Gerber-Chavez

Cindy began sewing as a young girl and has been sewing and quilting for more than 40 years. The passion for sewing and quilting has always been a large part of her life. Cindy quilted on a mid-arm machine until shortly after she retired from the FAA, at which time she purchased her Statler.

Cindy has quilted professionally since the beginning of 2012. She teaches free-motion quilting and Creative Studio classes at Rocky Mountain Quilting and Sewing. Cindy and her husband reside in Texas.

Paula Everill

Paula has devoted many years to her love of quilting, specializing in computerized custom quilting, heirloom quilting, piecing and heirloom sewing. She’s combined her expertise in computers with her passion for creating, culminating in the perfect union of skills on the longarm quilting machine. Paula’s talent in the classroom directly impacts her ability to present to a wide variety of students and consumers. Paula has been trained and certified in hand driving by Linda V Taylor.
