Our Team

Certified Statler Instructors

Cindy Gerber-Chavez

Cindy began sewing as a young girl and has been sewing and quilting for more than 40 years. The passion for sewing and quilting has always been a large part of her life. Cindy quilted on a mid-arm machine until shortly after she retired from the FAA, at which time she purchased her Statler.

Cindy has quilted professionally since the beginning of 2012. She teaches free-motion quilting and Creative Studio classes at Rocky Mountain Quilting and Sewing. Cindy and her husband reside in Utah. They have two children and three grandchildren.

Paula Everill

Paula has devoted many years to her love of quilting, specializing in computerized custom quilting, heirloom quilting, piecing and heirloom sewing. She’s combined her expertise in computers with her passion for creating, culminating in the perfect union of skills on the longarm quilting machine. Paula’s talent in the classroom directly impacts her ability to present to a wide variety of students and consumers. Paula works for and teaches at Rocky Mountain Electric Quilter, and has also been trained and certified in hand driving by Linda V Taylor.
