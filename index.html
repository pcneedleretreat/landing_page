Come and learn how to get the most out of your Gammill Statler.

Retreat: March 7-9, 2018

If you have any questions please call: 385-645-5401

Overview

We are happy to announce PC Needle Retreats Spring Retreat, March 7,8 & 9, 2018 in Riverton, Utah at the Old Dome Meeting Hall, 1452 W 12600 S, Riverton, UT 84065.

Joan Knight will join us and teach Creative Studio 7.0. Creative Studio 7.0 was released in August with some wonderful new features and enhancements.

The retreat will be a half day on Wednesday, March 7th, starting at 1pm with two full days of class on Thursday and Friday, March 8 & 9th. The retreat includes 2 ½ days of Statler training, dinner on Wednesday and Thursday evening, lunch on Thursday and Friday and lots of fun. As a holiday special the retreat rate will be reduced by $20.00 if you register by midnight, December 23, 2017. So add this fun retreat to your Christmas list and join us in March.

See you in March,

- Paula Everill and Cindy Gerber-Chavez

Instructors

Joan Knight is an award-winning quilter in regional and national quilt venues. She is a national and international Certified Statler Instructor in all 6 versions of Creative Studio. Credits include magazine and book related articles including an article in a Japanese quilting magazine. Her love of creating is fed daily by students and Creative Studio. Pushing Creative Studio to its limits inspires her to reach higher in her own quilting and teaching. For more information, please see her website.

Our Team

Certified Statler Instructors

Cindy Gerber-Chavez

Cindy began sewing as a young girl and has been sewing and quilting for more than 40 years. The passion for sewing and quilting has always been a large part of her life. Cindy quilted on a mid-arm machine until shortly after she retired from the FAA, at which time she purchased her Statler.

Cindy has quilted professionally since the beginning of 2012. She teaches free-motion quilting and Creative Studio classes. Cindy and her husband reside in Texas.

Paula Everill

Paula has devoted many years to her love of quilting, specializing in computerized custom quilting, heirloom quilting, piecing and heirloom sewing. She’s combined her expertise in computers with her passion for creating, culminating in the perfect union of skills on the longarm quilting machine. Paula’s talent in the classroom directly impacts her ability to present to a wide variety of students and consumers. Paula has been trained and certified in hand driving by Linda V Taylor.
